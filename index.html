Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Pomodoro is a simple productivity application designed to help users focus using timed work and break sessions.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Local Data

The app stores only basic preferences locally on the user's device such as:

  • Theme selection (light / dark / system)
  • Language preference (English / Turkish)

This data never leaves the device.

Internet Usage

The app does not require user accounts or backend servers. Any network usage is limited to essential system-level functions.

Children's Privacy

This app does not target children under 13 and does not collect any data from users.
